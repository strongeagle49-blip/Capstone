Welcome to my gallery

I have been drawing for about 20 years. Starting with traditional pen and paper, I have since moved on to digital art. I draw with an anime artstyle because shows and games with that artstyle have had a big influence on me

About my Art

I like to draw fanart of my favorite characters as well my original creations. What inspired me were older anime like .hack//sign and Full Metal Alchemist, the more nuanced story telling and appealing artstyle of those shows really drew me in and I wanted to draw in a similar style.
