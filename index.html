Welcome to my gallery

I have been drawing for about 20 years now. I used to spend hours after school doodling on blank paper, my note books but once I had a chance to draw on a art tablet I was hooked. Art has always been a huge part of my life and it's something I consistently do every day.

About Me

I like to draw fanart of my favorite characters as well my original creations. What inspired me were older anime like .hack//sign and Full Metal Alchemist, the stories and appealing artstyle of those shows really drew me in and I wanted to draw in a similar style. I also have a strong love for video games and my hobby happens to align with that as well. Some of my favorite artists like Yoshitaka Amano, Ayami Kojima, Ashley Wood and Yoji Shinkawa have done artwork for some of my favorite games.
